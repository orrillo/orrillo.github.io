I am a Research Fellow at the Institute for Applied Economic Research (IPEA).

I hold a PhD in Economics from FGV EPGE (2025). My research specializes in theoretical econometrics and mathematical modeling, with a core focus on the closure properties of linear processes and the emergence of non-ergodicity in macroeconometric structures.

I also investigate how sectoral interventions and input-output linkages shape economic development and the stability of asset pricing in long-run behavior.

Main Research Topics
  • • Theoretical econometrics: Closure of linear processes and non-ergodic limits.
  • • Production networks: Sectoral interventions and investment networks.
  • • Financial economics: Asymptotic arbitrage and hyperopic asset pricing.
